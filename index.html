Hi, I'm Satya Nadella


CEO of Microsoft


Let's Talk His Works

Satya Nadella, CEO of Microsoft since 2014, has significantly transformed the company with a focus on cloud computing, driving its stock value up nearly tenfold. He led major acquisitions, including Mojang, LinkedIn, and GitHub, and revamped Microsoft’s corporate culture to emphasize empathy and growth. Before becoming CEO, Nadella drove the shift to cloud infrastructure in his previous roles. He has received numerous accolades, such as Time 100 honors, Financial Times Person of the Year, and the Padma Bhushan. Nadella holds an honorary Ph.D. from Georgia Tech and is involved in various boards, including Starbucks and Fred Hutchinson Cancer Research Center. He is also a cricket fan and philanthropist, living in Washington with his wife, Anupama, and their three children. His son Zain’s special needs have deeply influenced his personal and professional outlook.
